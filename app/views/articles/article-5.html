Kevin Garnett and Tim Duncan are two of the greatest power forwards of all time, accounting for 14 1st Team All-NBA honors since the 1997-98 season. Duncan will forever be known for his five championships with the Spurs, Garnett for his intensity on the court and the banner he brought to Boston. These long, successful careers beg to be broken down against one another. I’ll analyze various parts of their careers, their ability to lead teams to regular season and playoff wins, and their impact on their teams ability to build a successful franchise.
